About the App

Women’s Euro Predictions 2025 is a simple form-based pick’em: no logins, no points tracked here. Fill in your scores, pick your Player of the Matchday and favourite team, and screenshot your ticket. This is a fan-driven, non-profit web app. All data is publicly available and we do not collect personal information. Built with ❤️.
