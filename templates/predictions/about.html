About Women’s Euro 2025 Predictions

This is a free, fan-driven web app where you can submit matchday score predictions, pick a Player of the Matchday, choose your favorite national team, and create a shareable ticket. No logins, no ads, no personal data collected.

How It Works

Predict the exact score for each match

Select your Player of the Matchday

Pick your favorite team

Screenshot your ticket and challenge friends

Data & Privacy

Fixtures, dates & venues from FBRef

Team logos & player names from UEFA official site

No registration or personal data storage

Non-profit, ad-free, built by volunteers

Built with ❤️ by a fan of the women’s game.
